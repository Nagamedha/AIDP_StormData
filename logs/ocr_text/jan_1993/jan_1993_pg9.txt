a 972 mb low over the gulf of alaska moved north along the gulf, pushing a strong occluded front
over southeast alaska with strong and gusty winds throughout the dle. sustained winds of
35 knots with gusts to 70 knots occurred, with the strongest in the southern panhandle. most
damage from this storm was from the heavy rain along with the warm temperatures (over 4 inches
of rain and 58 degrees, in se alaska). storm drains were clogged with the seasons snowpack.
several homes had flooded basements in ketchikan as a result, and a small bridge collapsed from
the weight. juneau experienced a temporary power and cable television outage, as a tree fell across
power lines, just in time for the super bowl.
